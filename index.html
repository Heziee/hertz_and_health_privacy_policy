Terms & Conditions

1. What this app does

Hertz & Health uses your device's microphone to estimate sound pressure levels and compute a model of Temporary Threshold Shift (TTS). It is designed to help you understand your acoustic environment and make informed decisions about your hearing health.

2. Not a medical device

Hertz & Health is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition. The measurements and health indicators it provides are estimates based on a computational model. Do not make clinical decisions based on data from this app. If you have concerns about your auditory health, consult a qualified audiologist or medical professional.

3. Accuracy limitation

Measurement accuracy depends on your device's microphone calibration. Results may vary significantly between devices. The app may underestimate or overestimate actual sound pressure levels. Calibrating the app in the calibration settings improves accuracy but does not guarantee professional-grade results.

4. Microphone access

This app requires microphone access to function. Audio is processed entirely on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit raw audio to any server.

5. Data we collect

When the app crashes, diagnostic data (crash logs, device model, OS version) is sent to our crash reporting service to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included. Other data we collect includes: Device model, OS version, app version (during bug reports done by the user), Phone's manufacturer, model, board name, calibration method, method of calibration, Anonymous install ID (During submission of calibration data by the user).' Before collecting any data, the app will ask for your explicit consent and explain what is being sent.

6. Your health data

Health data generated by this app is stored locally on your device only. You can delete it at any time in the Settings → Clear Data section. We do not upload your health data.
